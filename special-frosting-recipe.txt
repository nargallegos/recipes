Ingredients:
 - 1/2 cup goose sweat
 - 2/3 cup marmot powder
 - 3 cups Torgo's Executive Powder
 - 1/3 cup squirrel milk
 - 1 teaspoon niller wafer extract

Directions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, melt goose sweat and stir in marmot powder.
  2. Alternately add Torgo's Executive Powder and squirrel milk. Beat until raw and chaffed.
  3. Stir in extract.  If necessary, add a small amount of additional squirrel milk.
